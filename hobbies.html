Gaming

Gaming is by far the largest of my hobbies. I play a myriad of games, ranging from competitive tactical shooters to strategy games. The two most important parts of a game for me are it's depth and how challenging it is. I play games that force me to think and solve problems, because that's what I love doing. To this day, no game has pushed me harder than Path of Exile. Despite playing for over a decade, it was only a few years ago that I finally beat...almost, every piece of content the game has to offer.
